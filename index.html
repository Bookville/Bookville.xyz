GAMES!

Games that are on our server.

CREATIVE

In the Creative World you have unlimited rescources and a default 75 by 75 blocks plot. If you wish for more plots you can get ranks like, Creator, Imagineer, and Dreamer. The creative world lets you show people your builds and creativity. Anything is possible in the creative world. Ranks like Creator, Imagineer, and Dreamer allow you to use world-edit and more plots. for more information about the Creative world go to the Bookville wiki. If you have any questions go to the bookNation discord forum.

SKYBLOCK

In the Skyblock world you will get the ability to thrive and grow your personal island. With shops and islands to explore the adventure keeps on going. Sell items and trade to get resources and money. This isn't your average skyblock world, with custom crafting and items there's more you can do. Like the creative world you can get ranks to get special stuff. With ranks like Scavenger, Island Hopper, and Master, you can do more then others. For more information about the Skyblock world go to the Bookville wiki. If you have any questions go to the bookNation discord forum.

SURVIVAL

In the Survival world you are put at a random cordinate on the world where you will attemp to survive and thrive. You can join with other players and make a community, PVP is alwaysed allowed to make the game more challenging. For more information go to the Bookville wiki. If you have any questions go to the bookNation discord forum.
